WAGERING FORMATS & OUTLETS

  • Sports Wagering – H2H & Battle Court

    Betting on jai-alai is executed through an exclusive North American arrangement with BetRivers (Rush Street Interactive). The locations where jai-alai may be wagered on include: Arizona, Colorado, Illinois, Iowa, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Virginia, Province of Ontario, and Mexico. Discussions are underway with Kambi and XB Net to expand sports wagering outside of North America.

  • Parimutuel Format

    Wagering on parimutuel play currently takes place in Florida at licensed parimutuel facilities, in Connecticut through the Connecticut OTB system, in Mexico through sportsbook “Caliente,” and online in states where legal through ADW company “WatchandWager.” Discussions are underway with XB Net to expand parimutuel wagering and offer fixed odds pari-mutuel wagering internationally.

  • Fantasy Sports

    The League currently has a non-exclusive arrangement with PrizePicks on parimutuel games. Fantasy will be a point of focus for expansion as the league continues to develop.
